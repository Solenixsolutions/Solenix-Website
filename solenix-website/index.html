    Who we are

    About Solenix Solutions

    Building Confidence. Supporting Clean Energy.

    Solenix Solutions is a manufacturer of solar mounting systems and accessories based in Jalandhar, Punjab, supplying EPC companies, distributors and solar professionals across India.

    Headquartered in Jalandhar, Punjab, Solenix Solutions was founded with a clear vision — to deliver reliable solar mounting systems and accessories that set the benchmark for quality, precision, and performance.

    As India's solar industry continues to grow, we remain committed to manufacturing products that empower installers, EPC companies, distributors, and project developers to build safer, stronger, and more efficient solar power systems.

    Every component we manufacture reflects our dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and uncompromising quality. We believe that every successful solar installation begins with a strong foundation, and our mission is to provide solutions that stand the test of time.

    From Jalandhar, Punjab, we are proud to contribute to India's clean energy journey by delivering products that inspire confidence and support a more sustainable future.
